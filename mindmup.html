Mind Mapping Tool

Of course, using Bright Sparks will lead you to generate lots of ideas. You might choose to record them on post-it notes, on a whiteboard, or in a software tool of your own.

But if you want to combine Bright Sparks with visual thinking, we recommend MindMup, a great open-source visual mind mapping tool. Click the button to open MindMup in a second window, and get creating.
